Privacy Policy

FieldReport does not collect, store, or share personal data.

All reports, photos, and notes created in the app are stored locally on the user’s device. The app does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Collection

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No third-party data sharing

Camera and Photos

FieldReport uses the device camera and photo library only to allow users to create work reports with photos. Photos are saved locally on the device and remain under the user’s control.

Purchases

All subscriptions and payments are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. FieldReport does not collect or have access to payment information.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated if required. Any changes will be published on this page.
